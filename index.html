About Us

Purpose of the Platform

The MUBS ICT Ministry Service Centre is designed to provide comprehensive support and resources for students and staff at MUBS. Our platform aims to streamline access to essential services, facilitate seamless communication, and enhance the overall university experience. Whether you need assistance with e-learning, managing your student portal, or contacting the ICT support team, our platform is here to assist you efficiently and effectively.

About the 27th MUBS Guild ICT Ministry

The 27th MUBS Guild ICT Ministry is dedicated to advancing the technological infrastructure of the university. Our mission is to improve digital services, ensure robust support for students and faculty, and drive innovation in technology use across campus. We are committed to providing top-notch ICT services, including technical support, digital resource management, and ensuring that all technological needs are met efficiently.

Software Assistance

Our Software Support Services

At the MUBS ICT Ministry Service Centre, we are committed to ensuring that students have access to the necessary software tools for their academic and personal needs. Our software support services include:

  • Software Installation and Configuration:

    Assistance with installing and configuring essential software required for coursework, research, and other academic activities.

  • Licensing and Access:

    Guidance on obtaining and managing software licenses, including access to educational discounts and free software provided by the university.

  • Troubleshooting and Support:

    Support for resolving issues related to software functionality, compatibility, and updates.

  • Training and Tutorials:

    Providing training sessions and tutorials to help students effectively use software tools and applications relevant to their studies.

  • Software Recommendations:

    Recommending suitable software solutions based on academic requirements and personal needs.

How to Access Software Assistance

To access our software assistance services, students can:

  • Visit the ICT Ministry Office:

    Drop by our office for in-person support and consultations.

  • Submit a Request Online:

    Use our online support request form available on our website to get help with software issues.

  • Attend Workshops:

    Participate in our scheduled workshops and training sessions on various software tools.

  • Contact Us via WhatsApp:

    Reach out to our support team directly through WhatsApp for quick assistance and queries.

We strive to ensure that all students have the resources they need to succeed in their academic pursuits. Our dedicated team is here to assist you with any software-related questions or challenges you may face.
