About Us

Purpose of the Platform

The MUBS ICT Ministry Service Centre is designed to provide comprehensive support and resources for students and staff at MUBS. Our platform aims to streamline access to essential services, facilitate seamless communication, and enhance the overall university experience. Whether you need assistance with e-learning, managing your student portal, or contacting the ICT support team, our platform is here to assist you efficiently and effectively.

About the 27th MUBS Guild ICT Ministry

The 27th MUBS Guild ICT Ministry is dedicated to advancing the technological infrastructure of the university. Our mission is to improve digital services, ensure robust support for students and faculty, and drive innovation in technology use across campus. We are committed to providing top-notch ICT services, including technical support, digital resource management, and ensuring that all technological needs are met efficiently.

Software Assistance

Our Software Support Services

At the MUBS ICT Ministry Service Centre, we are committed to ensuring that students have access to the necessary software tools for their academic and personal needs. Our software support services include:

  • Software Installation and Configuration: Assistance with installing and configuring essential software required for coursework, research, and other academic activities.
  • Licensing and Access: Guidance on obtaining and managing software licenses, including access to educational discounts and free software provided by the university.
  • Troubleshooting and Support: Support for resolving issues related to software functionality, compatibility, and updates.
  • Training and Tutorials: Providing training sessions and tutorials to help students effectively use software tools and applications relevant to their studies.
  • Software Recommendations: Recommending suitable software solutions based on academic requirements and personal needs.

How to Access Software Assistance

To access our software assistance services, students can:

  • Visit the ICT Ministry Office: Drop by our office for in-person support and consultations.
  • Submit a Request Online: Use our online support request form available on our website to get help with software issues.
  • Attend Workshops: Participate in our scheduled workshops and training sessions on various software tools.
  • Contact Us via WhatsApp: Reach out to our support team directly through WhatsApp for quick assistance and queries.

We strive to ensure that all students have the resources they need to succeed in their academic pursuits. Our dedicated team is here to assist you with any software-related questions or challenges you may face.

Step-by-Step Procedures

Comprehensive guidance on using MUBSEP e-learning platform, MUBS Students Portal, attending live classes, and managing tuition payments.

MUBSEP Portal

Follow these steps to navigate the MUBSEP e-learning platform:

1. Accessing the Platform

Visit the MUBSEP website and log in using your student credentials. If you’re logging in for the first time, use your student webmail (e.g., 2400700000@mubs.ca.ug) and the word 'password' as your Password. Be sure to reset your password to a preferred strong password immediately after logging in.

2. Navigating the Dashboard

Once logged in, explore the dashboard for an overview of your courses, notifications, and upcoming deadlines.

3. Accessing Course Materials

Select a course to access lecture notes, reading materials, and other resources provided by your instructors.

4. Participating in Course Activities

Submit assignments, participate in discussion forums, and complete quizzes and exams online.

5. Tracking Your Progress

Check your grades and feedback, and monitor your progress through course materials and activities.

6. Communication

Use the platform’s messaging system and read announcements for important updates.

7. Additional Features

View important dates on the integrated calendar and access support resources if needed.

8. Logging Out

When finished, log out to ensure your account’s security.

MUBS Students Portal

Follow these steps for accessing and utilizing the MUBS Students Portal:

1. Logging In

Go to the MUBS Students Portal and log in with your student ID and password. If you’re new, use your initial credentials provided by the administration and change your password immediately.

2. Managing Your Profile

Update your personal details and contact information, and upload any required documents.

3. Registering for Courses

Select and register for courses offered in the current semester.

4. Viewing Grades

Check your academic performance and view your grades for completed courses.

5. Accessing Financial Information

Review your fee statements and make payments online.

6. Requesting Transcripts

Order academic transcripts and other certificates as needed.

7. Communicating with Faculty

Send messages to faculty members and attend virtual office hours if available.

8. Logging Out

Ensure you log out from the portal to protect your personal information.
